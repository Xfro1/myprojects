Our Services

Event Planning and Management

We have extensive experience in planning and managing events of all types and sizes, including conferences, seminars, and more. We take care of every aspect of the event, from venue selection and vendor management to guest registration and on-site support.

Translation and Interpretation Services

Our team of experienced translators and interpreters provide accurate and reliable translation services in a variety of languages. We can provide simultaneous, consecutive, and whisper interpretation, and translate documents, websites, and other materials, as well as provide on-site interpretation services.

Technical Support

We provide technical support for all types of events, including sound and lighting, video production, simultaneous interpretation equipment, Zoom-related equipment, and more. Our team of technicians is experienced in setting up and managing technical equipment to ensure that your event runs smoothly.

Exceptional Catering for Every Occasion

At Mesopotamia, we take pride in delivering high-quality catering services tailored to your needs. Whether you’re hosting a corporate event, conference, workshop, or any special gathering, our expert team ensures a seamless and delicious experience. We offer a diverse menu featuring fresh, high-quality ingredients, customizable options, and exceptional presentation. From gourmet buffets to elegant plated meals and convenient boxed lunches, we cater to events of all sizes and styles. Our commitment to outstanding service, attention to detail, and culinary excellence makes us the perfect partner for your catering needs.

Reliable Procurement Solutions for Your Business

We specialize in providing efficient and cost-effective procurement solutions tailored to meet your business needs. Whether you require sourcing high-quality products, managing supplier relationships, or optimizing your supply chain, our experienced team ensures a seamless process from start to finish. Our procurement services include strategic sourcing, supplier management, contract negotiation, inventory & logistics support, and cost optimization. With a focus on transparency, efficiency, and value, we help businesses streamline procurement, minimize risks, and maximize savings.

Seamless Travel & Event Booking Services

At Mesopotamia, we take the hassle out of booking flights, accommodations, and event venues. Whether you're planning a business trip, corporate event, or private gathering, our team ensures a smooth and stress-free experience with tailored solutions to fit your needs. Our services include flight bookings, hotel & accommodation reservations, event venue sourcing, group travel coordination, and custom travel & event packages. With our expertise and industry connections, we guarantee convenience, cost-effectiveness, and a smooth planning process.
