About Us

Mesopotamia is a professional event management and translation services company headquartered in Iraq. With years of experience in the industry, we pride ourselves on delivering top-quality services that meet the needs of our clients. Our highly skilled team of professionals includes event planners, interpreters, translators, and technicians who work together to ensure the success of every event we organize.

We believe that communication is the key to success, which is why we work closely with our clients to ensure that their needs are met at every stage of the event planning and management process.

We have worked with local and international clients such as the World Bank, UN agencies, TAG International, ICMPD, European delegations, Embassies, and Ministries. Our team is committed to providing exceptional service and ensuring that every event we organize is a success.
